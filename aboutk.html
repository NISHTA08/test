ABOUT KitKat

KitKat is a perfect balance of chocolate and wafer first launched in the UK in 1935 as 'Chocolate Crisp'. The KitKat brand name and logo was introduced on pack in 1937. Today, the iconic brand is present in more than 80 countries. The famous Have a break, have a KitKat strapline was first used in 1958 and has been used without a 'break' ever since. KitKat Chocolatory is the ultimate KitKat Break Experience offering products with unique premium flavours, personalized and customized, digitally shareable. KitKat is the first global confectionery brand to be sourced from 100% sustainable cocoa.
